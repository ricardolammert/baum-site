Privacy Policy

Baum LLC · Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Baum LLC ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact our business, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

When you call or text our business, we collect the information you provide — such as your name, phone number, the details of the work you are requesting, and the content of your messages. We do not collect sensitive personal information.

How we use it

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry: to answer your questions, prepare a price estimate, schedule and confirm appointments, and follow up regarding the service you requested.

Text messaging

By contacting us, you consent to receive SMS text messages from us in reply to your inquiry. Message frequency varies based on your interaction with us. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance.

We do not share your information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information — including your mobile phone number, SMS consent, or opt-in data — with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing. Information is used only to provide the service you contacted us about.

Data retention & security

We retain your information only as long as needed to serve you and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable measures to protect it.
