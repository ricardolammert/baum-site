SMS Terms & Conditions

Baum LLC · Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Program

Baum LLC operates a customer messaging program. When you contact our business by phone or text, we reply by SMS to answer your questions, provide price estimates, and schedule appointments for the services you request.

How to join

You opt in by contacting us first — calling or texting our business number. We only send messages to people who have reached out to us. We do not send marketing messages.

Message frequency & cost

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with us. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out and help

You can cancel at any time by replying STOP. After you send STOP, we will send one confirmation message and will not message you again unless you contact us anew. For help, reply HELP or email us.

Privacy

Your information is handled per our Privacy Policy. We do not share mobile information or SMS consent with third parties for marketing.
